User Research

Research begins with defining the problem, ideation and brainstorming. Through stakeholder and user interviews, thoughts are garnered on how to improve the current version of a website or app, or begin with the creation of a new a product.

The design process begins after interviews have been conducted, and the problem has been defined. Beginning with wireframes, and moving into low fidelity mock-ups. Once these have been approved, the high fidelity phase commences.

Web and app development begins after high fidelity prototypes have been tested and approved, followed by changes and testing. The end result is then presented for approval to go "live" or delivered to a developer for further progression.
